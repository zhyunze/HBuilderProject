About the college

Shijiazhuang Posts and Telecommunications Technical College was founded in 1956. It is an institution of higher learning affiliated to China Post Group, the Chinese Communist Party School of China Post Group, the Training Center of China Post Group, the Postal School of the Open University of China, the National Center of China Post Online College and the scientific research base of China Post Group.

Shijazhuang Posts and Telecommunications Technical College has three campuses (the new campus located in Zheng Ding County under construction], covering a total area of over 603 ,000 square metres. It has excellent teaching and training infrastructures, with a building area of over 250 ,000 square metres . The college has Modern Post, Intelligent Communication, Finance and Accounting with thirty majors, 73 on-campus training rooms, over 8,000 full timne students. It provides centralized training to more than 28,000 people and distance training to about 3,000,000 people annually and supports or conducts over 100 research projects every year.
